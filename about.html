We Love Each Other

Our Story

Step into the chapters of our love, and feel the journey that brought us here.

  • First We Meet

    March 29, 2023 @ Big Joes

    March 26, 2023 — I had just finished taking the Civil Service exam, and while I was resting, my friend Kevin suddenly messaged me, telling me to chat with someone. I couldn’t see much of her Facebook profile since it was locked, but something about her already caught my heart. When she replied to me, that’s where it all began. The very next day, Monday, we agreed to go jogging at the boulevard, and I picked her up in front of Joe’s Chicken Inato. From that moment on, we found ourselves meeting every evening, night after night, as if the universe itself was drawing us closer together..

  • First Date

    April 2, 2023 @ Dipolog Cathedral

    Night after night we met, until one evening I asked her to go to church with me on Sunday. I was excited and overjoyed, because we were both Catholics who cherished the Sunday Mass. It turned out to be Palm Sunday, and there we were, standing side by side, holding palm branches as if it were a quiet blessing upon us. After Mass, she allowed me to take her home to Roxas, ZN, where she introduced me to her family. I was overjoyed to discover that our parents already knew each other—it felt as though fate had woven our paths long before we even realized it. From that day on, I devoted myself to courting her every single day, my heart certain that she was the one I wanted to spend my life with.

  • In A Relationship

    April 10, 2023

    When we agreed to go on a date in Mutia, ZN, we went to Gantao Hill. There, we wandered happily together, and even though the sky threatened heavy rain, nothing could stop us—we continued, laughing and savoring every moment.
    Later that day, we visited my mother, who was confined in the hospital with a fever. It was then that I saw how deeply she cared—not only for me, but also for my family, especially my mama. That moment touched me beyond words. When we finally returned home, she gave me the sweetest answer—I became hers, and she became mine. That was the beginning of our love story, the moment our hearts chose each other. And now, here we stand, ready to make the most beautiful decision of our lives: to marry and share forever together.
